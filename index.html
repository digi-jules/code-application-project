An app to keep your digital mental health resources in
one place.

OTWYFS is a concept for a new app on a mission to help you keep your personal mental health and well-being resources at your fingertips, regardless of whether you suffer from mental illness or not. It is always a good idea to be prepared for the worst, and OTWYFS provides you with a simple way of accessing all your favorite mood-boosters (like photos and notes), online mental health resources, and a list of trusted contacts as well as crisis help.

Your mental health is important.

Mental illness can be severely debilitating. Prepare yourself in advance for a variety of mental health related situations, so you can focus on getting better and making the most of your life. Here are just some of the many reasons to take control of your mental health today:

Depression is one of the leading causes of disability worldwide.

Mental health problems are increasing.

Anyone can develop a mental illness at some point in life.

Mental illness can impair cognitive functioning.

Features

bulletin board

An interactive way to keep all sorts of your favorite short notes and media files together. Photos, lists, links to a video or song, affirmations, notes from loved ones - customize your bulletin board with everything that grounds you.

digital resources

Compile online mental health resources like guides with coping strategies or portals for finding a therapist. OTWYFS comes preloaded with a list of credible resources based on your location, but feel free to add your own!

reach out

One of the best ways to deal with negative thoughts and feelings is to talk about them with trusted people in your life. Keep a list of contacts who you feel comfortable talking about your mental health with.

crisis help

Access crisis hotlines and textlines quickly and easily. List emergency medical contacts. Keep a document with your personal crisis plan and/or any other resources that could be of help in an emergency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to have a diagnosed mental illness to use OTWYFS?

No, you do not need to have a diagnosed mental illness, nor do you even have to be feeling sad. OTWYFS's fully-customizable features can be utilized by anyone who wants to have a convenient way to care for their mental health. Even if you feel your mental health is in perfect shape, it is still a good idea to have some resources ready in case things were to take a turn for the worse.

Does OTWYFS function as a treatment for mental illness?

No. OTWYFS is a tool that is meant to be used alongside treatment (in users that require it). Please do not use OTWYFS instead of seeking professional help for your mental illness.

How is OTWYFS different from similar apps?

The main distinguising feature is the personalized virtual "bulletin board" that you will have with you everywhere you go. Post anything and everything that brings you. Also, all features are fully customizable, meaning this app can be tailored to your specific needs.

Is my personal information secure with OTWYFS?

Yes, your personal info is safe! We understand that medical data is especially sensitive, and it will never under any circumstances be sold or shared.

About

The idea for OTWYFS was born while I was browsing my notes app and rediscovered a meaningful list from the past, titled "open this when you're feeling sad..." The note includes a list of people I love, positive affirmations, reasons to live, etc., and it was written during a dark period of my life as a way to remind myself that I was not alone and things will get better. This list was my lifeline at times, especially when I felt my mental health was heading into a downwards spiral.

I think many others could benefit from the concept of having a personalized list of ways to cope with their struggles, and I now hope to one day have the resources to make the development of this app a reality.
